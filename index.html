Frequently asked

Useful answers before you contact us.

What products do you supply?+

Our core range includes concrete blocks, interlock paving tiles and concrete kerbstones for construction, paving and landscaping applications.

Can I ask for product sizes and availability?+

Yes. Contact the team with the product, quantity and intended use. They can discuss current options and availability with you.

Do you serve residential and commercial projects?+

Our cement products are suitable for a range of project types. Share your specific requirement so the team can guide you to an appropriate option.

How do I request a quote?+

Call or WhatsApp +968 9417 8871. Include your product type, required dimensions, estimated quantity and project location for a more useful response.
